# Use Cases

Potential use cases for the CISA KEV & NVD vulnerability research chatbot.

## Security Operations

### Vulnerability Triage

Analysts query the chatbot to prioritize patching based on CVSS scores,
known ransomware campaign usage, and CISA remediation due dates. Natural
language questions like "Which critical vulnerabilities are due this week?"
surface actionable results instantly.

### Compliance Reporting

Generate lists of KEV vulnerabilities affecting specific vendors or products
for audit and compliance purposes. Useful for demonstrating adherence to
BOD 22-01 or internal patch management policies.

### Incident Response

During an active incident, responders can quickly look up CVE details,
affected products, required actions, and cross-reference KEV and NVD data
without manually navigating multiple government websites.

### Vendor Risk Assessment

Query vulnerabilities by vendor to evaluate third-party software risk.
Identify which vendors have the most known exploited vulnerabilities and
whether those vulnerabilities have been used in ransomware campaigns.

## Broader Adoption

### CISO & Executive Briefings

Non-technical stakeholders can ask natural language questions about the
organization's exposure landscape without needing to understand SQL or
navigate raw vulnerability feeds.

### DevSecOps Integration

Developers and release engineers check whether project dependencies have
known exploited vulnerabilities before shipping, supporting shift-left
security practices.

### Security Awareness & Training

New analysts can use the chatbot as a learning tool to explore the
vulnerability landscape, understand CVSS scoring, and familiarize
themselves with CISA KEV catalog entries.

### Audit & Evidence Gathering

Security teams compile evidence of vulnerability awareness and remediation
timelines for internal or external audits by querying historical KEV data.
